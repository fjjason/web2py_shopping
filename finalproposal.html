Description

This is a visualization that covers the more common wine, testified by wine tasting expert groups. Vertical scale will be the price of the wine, the top side means more expensive. The Horizontal scale will be the taste of the wine, the right side means better tasting experience. By hover over, tooltip will be used to display the name of the wine, place of origin, and a short description.
